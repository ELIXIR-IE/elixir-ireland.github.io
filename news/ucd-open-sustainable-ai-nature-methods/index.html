UCD co-authors contribute to Nature Methods paper on Open and Sustainable AI

A strong ELIXIR Ireland contribution to a pan-European publication in Nature Methods.

2 April 2026

Denis Shields and Raúl Fernández-Díaz of University College Dublin, an ELIXIR Ireland member institute, are co-authors on a new Nature Methods paper on open and sustainable AI.

The publication brings together expertise from across the ELIXIR community, with contributions from more than 10 ELIXIR nodes. It highlights pan-European guidance for developing and applying AI in life sciences and health in ways that are open, responsible, and sustainable.

This is a strong example of ELIXIR Ireland’s role in shaping shared European best practice and contributing to high-impact international outputs.
