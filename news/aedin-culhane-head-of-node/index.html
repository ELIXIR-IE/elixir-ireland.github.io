ELIXIR Ireland Welcomes New Head of Node Aedín Culhane

A new leadership chapter for ELIXIR Ireland, with sincere thanks to Denis Shields and Colm Ryan for their stewardship.

1 June 2025

ELIXIR Ireland is pleased to welcome Prof. Aedín Culhane of the University of Limerick as the new Head of Node.

Aedín is Professor of Cancer Genomics and Director of the Limerick Digital Cancer Research Centre. She brings deep experience in cancer genomics, biostatistics, and data-driven biomedical research, along with a strong record of collaborative leadership across research communities.

Her appointment will help strengthen ELIXIR Ireland’s role in connecting national expertise in bioinformatics, data stewardship, and life science infrastructure with the wider European ELIXIR network.

As Aedín steps into this role, ELIXIR Ireland also extends its warmest thanks and recognition to Denis Shields and Colm Ryan for their previous leadership, stewardship, and sustained efforts in guiding the node. Their commitment and vision have helped build the strong foundation on which ELIXIR Ireland continues to grow.

We look forward to this next phase of development under Aedín’s leadership and to the continued strengthening of the Irish bioinformatics community.
