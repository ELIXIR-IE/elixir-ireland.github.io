Lars Jermiin featured in Dyslexia Ireland profile

Lars Jermiin, ELIXIR Ireland Research Data Management Coordinator at the University of Galway, is featured in a profile by Dyslexia Ireland.

26 May 2026

Lars Jermiin, Research Data Management Coordinator for ELIXIR Ireland at the University of Galway, has been featured in a profile by Dyslexia Ireland.

Lars is an Assistant Professor at the University of Galway with expertise in research data management, computational biology, and scientific writing and editing. In his role as RDM Coordinator, he supports the Irish life science research community in managing and sharing data in line with FAIR principles — a vital contribution to the ELIXIR Ireland Node.

ELIXIR Ireland is proud to have Lars as part of the team and delighted to see his work and story recognised beyond the research community.
