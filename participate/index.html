Participate

Get involved with ELIXIR Ireland — whether you're at a member institute or want to bring your organisation on board

How to Get Involved

ELIXIR Ireland welcomes researchers, bioinformaticians, data stewards, and anyone working in the life sciences across Ireland. How you participate depends on whether your institution is already a member.

I'm at a Member Institute

If you're a researcher, student, or staff member at one of our member institutes, you can join the ELIXIR Ireland community right away. Sign up for our mailing list and request an onboarding pack to get started.

Learn more ↓

I Want My Institute to Join

Membership is free for Irish research-performing organisations. The process involves a formal application approved by our Management Board. We provide an information pack and expression of interest form to get things started.

Bring Your Institute on Board

Membership is free for Irish research-performing organisations. The process requires approval from the ELIXIR Ireland Management Board and involves a formal application coordinated with your institutional leads.

How it works: Download the information pack below for full details on benefits, responsibilities, and the application process. When ready, submit an expression of interest via the form and we'll guide you through the next steps.
